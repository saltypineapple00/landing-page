Sprout

2D platformer game called Sprout! A simple platformer game where the player plays a lead creature going through the vast forest towards while collecting water droplets and encounter enemies and obstacles. Player will encounter moving, attacking enemies and/or obstacles. Player is able to interact with different platforms in the games that are moving, passable and so on. I worked as a programmer for this project of 14 people and worked on platform, obstacles and enemy movements and behaviors.
